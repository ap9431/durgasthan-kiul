Durga Asthan a temple in Kiul was established in 1942 before independence.
Here,the month of January is celebrated every year as a festival. Because Durga Maa's statue was established in this holy month.It is located in Kiul a village of Lakhisarai district of Bihar. During Durga Puja we can see a huge gathering from other neighbouring villages and districts. Its place of worship for one and all and temple welcomes each one with it's open gates . The local communal as well as youth both remain the main focus of the temples trust in collaboration with other institutions of faith peace and unity are pillars of the temple.
